FREQUENTLY ASKED // CLASSIFIED

Questions.

Can I pay extra for revisions?

No — but your quiz will be very easy to edit yourself if you'd like to make changes after delivery.

Will all my results pages be built out on my website?

Yes, if you'd like them to be.

Do I need a specific kind of website host?

Yes. I work in all-in-one platforms and hosts like Squarespace, Showit, and similar. I don't work in WordPress.

What if I don't have a website?

No problem — I'll build everything out directly in Interact for you.

What does this actually cost me in total?

The Quiz Oracle package is $500. You'll also need an Interact account — their lead-collection plan runs $39/month or $27/month billed annually. If you already have an email service provider, that's it. If you don't, you'll need one of those too.

Do I need an email service provider?

Yes — to actually collect and store your leads, you'll need an ESP (think Flodesk, Mailchimp, Kit, etc.). If you're already paying for one, you're good. I'll connect it to your Interact account as part of the build, as long as you give me access beforehand.

Will you set up my email sequences or automations?

I'll connect your ESP to Interact so your leads flow in automatically — but setting up any email sequences or automations on your ESP side is outside the scope of this package.

What's the Big Reveal email?

Once your quiz is live, you'll want to announce it. Included in your package is a Big Reveal email you can send to your existing audience to launch your new quiz. Think of it as the party announcement for the thing I just built you.

Is Interact free?

Interact has a free 14-day trial, but to collect leads and connect your ESP, you'll need a paid plan. Their pricing starts at $39/month or $27/month on an annual plan. This is separate from your $500 investment with me.

What ESP do you recommend?

Totally up to you — I'll work with whatever you're already using. If you're starting from scratch, pick whatever fits your budget and needs. I don't have a preference as long as it integrates with Interact (most major ones do).

Why don't I get to see the quiz before it's delivered, and why are there no revisions?

Because that's the offer. This is an intuition-led, bespoke build — the magic is in the trust. When you hand me the keys and let me work without interruption, you get something that's been built entirely on instinct and expertise, not by committee. Revision rounds and previews would change the whole nature of what this is. The good news? I'm the Quiz Queen. I've done this enough times to know what works — and Interact makes it genuinely easy to tweak things yourself after delivery if something doesn't feel right.
